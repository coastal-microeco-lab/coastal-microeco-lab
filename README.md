## 👋 Hello world! We are the Coastal Microbial & Ecosystem Ecology Lab
Welcome to our GitHub page. We are a group of scientists passionate about microbial communities in coastal ecosystems. 
- 🌊 We research marine, estuarine, and wetland microbial ecology and responses to human-driven environmental change.
- 🦪 Sometimes we sequence other things, too, like oyster, rat, and bat microbes
- 🌱 Always interested in new ideas related to microbes, greenhouse gases, plant-microbe interactions, & biogeochemical cycling. Let's collaborate!
- 📚 Currently improving our bioinformatic and analytical skills. Follow along for tutorials and scripts used in Coastal MicroEco Lab manuscripts and workflows!
- 📫 Questions? Email us at: coastal.microeco.lab@gmail.com
